Nutrition Facts:

Calories:527kcal

Carbs: 39g

Protein: 52g

Fat: 27g

Nutrition Facts:

Carbs: 39g

Protein: 12g

Fat: 15g

Recipe Directions:

Heat oil in a skillet, cook kale until wilted (1-2 minutes), then add garlic and crushed red pepper, cooking for 30 seconds.

Easy Pea & Spinach Carbonara

Nutrition Facts:

Calories:479kcal

Carbs: 51g

Protein: 20g

Fat: 23g

Recipe Directions:

Heat oil in a skillet, add breadcrumbs and garlic; cook, stirring, until toasted...

Add beans and cook until rice is tender, about 5 more minutes.

Add spinach and peas, cook until pasta is tender, about 1 minute more.

Gradually add mixture to pasta, tossing to combine.
